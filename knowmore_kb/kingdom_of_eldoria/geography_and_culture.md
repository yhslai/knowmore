### Regional Divisions

#### Mountain Region (North)
- Dominated by the Ironcrag Mountains
- Population density: 28 people/km²
- Major cities: **Zephyris**, **Altair**, **Frostvale**
- Economy: Mining, timber, hydropower

#### Interior Plains (Central)
- Agricultural heartland
- Population density: 156 people/km²
- Major cities: **Elden City** (capital), **Riverton**, **Grainport**
- Economy: Agriculture, manufacturing

#### Coastal Lowlands (South)
- Fertile maritime zone
- Population density: 284 people/km²
- Major cities: **Port Alder**, **Seastrider**, **Merchant's Bay**
- Economy: Trade, fishing, tourism

## Cultural Traditions

### The Festival of Seven Tapers
Held annually on the first full moon after the spring equinox:

| Day | Activity | Location | Duration |
|-----|----------|----------|----------|
| 1 | Opening Ceremony | Elden City | 3 hours |
| 2-6 | Regional Celebrations | Each Canton | 8 hours/day |
| 7 | Closing Ritual | Elden City | 4 hours |

Traditions:
- Each Taper receives a symbolic honor
- Citizens wear traditional garments
- Public storytelling of national history
- Cultural exchanges between regions

### Cuisine

**National Dish**: **Tapestry Stew**
- A symbolic stew representing all Taper domains
- Contains grains, vegetables, meat, and herbs
- Prepared in special vessels with seven decorative elements

**Food Categories by Region**:

| Region | Signature Dish | Ingredients |
|--------|----------------|-------------|
| Mountain | Ironcrag Bread | Dark grain, honeyed butter |
| Interior | Silvervein Pasta | Wheat, river fish, herbs |
| Coastal | Seafoam Tart | Sea vegetables, shellfish |
| All | Harmony Tea | Seven different herbs |

### Traditional Festivities

#### The Lantern Festival (Winter)
Occurs during the new moon before the winter solstice:

$$ M_{festival} = \lfloor \frac{N_{days}}{365} \rfloor \text{ new moon} $$

Citizens release paper lanterns symbolizing hopes for the following year:
- Red lanterns: Health and strength
- Green lanterns: Growth and prosperity
- Gold lanterns: Wealth and success

#### The River Ceremony (Summer)
Held when the Silvervein reaches peak flow:

| Element | Purpose | Participants |
|---------|---------|--------------|
| Boat Procession | Honor river spirits | 100+ boats |
| Flower Release | Cleanse the water | Children |
| Music Ceremony | Celebrate community | All citizens |
| Feast | Share harvest | Families |

## Education System

### Structure
Eldorian education follows a four-tier model:

```
Tier 1: Primary (Ages 6-12)  = 6 years
Tier 2: Secondary (Ages 12-17) = 5 years
Tier 3: Pre-University (Ages 17-19) = 2 years
Tier 4: University (Ages 19-22+) = Variable
```

## National Symbols

### The Great Emblem

**Elements:**
$$ S = \{ \text{Tapers (7), Crown, Charter, Light} \} $$

- **Seven Taper Symbols**: Represent Council members
- **Central Crown**: Represents the monarchy
- **Eternal Flaming Torch**: Represents knowledge and guidance
- **Blue Field**: Represents the sky and heavens

### National Anthem
Title: **"The Light That Illuminates"**
- Composed in 1247
- Written by First Taper poet **Valmir the Wise**
- Sung during official ceremonies and major festivals
