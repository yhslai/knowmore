## Currency System

### The Crown Mark (CM)
The Crown Mark is subdivided into 100 **Cents** and divided by **Tapers** (10 Tapers = 1 CM):

$$ 1 \text{ CM} = 100 \text{ Cents} = 1000 \text{ Tapers} = 10000 \text{ Dots} $$

**Physical Forms**:
- Copper (Dots): Daily small transactions
- Silver (Tapers/Cents): Standard transactions
- Gold (Marks): Large purchases and savings

### The Harmony Bond
A unique investment instrument unique to Eldoria where citizens can invest in national projects:

$$ R_i = \frac{P_t}{P_i} \times \left(1 + \frac{H_t - H_i}{100}\right) $$

Where:
- $P_i$: Purchase price
- $P_t$: Redemption price
- $H_t$: Harmony Index at redemption
- $H_i$: Harmony Index at purchase

## Sector Breakdown

### Agriculture (35% of GDP)
The largest economic sector, governed by **Land Tenure Law**:

- **Small Holdings (< 10 hectares)**: 60% of farms
- **Medium Holdings (10-50 hectares)**: 30% of farms
- **Large Estates (> 50 hectares)**: 10% of farms

### Industry and Manufacturing (28% of GDP)
Key industries include:

| Industry | Output Value | Key Products |
|----------|--------------|--------------|
| Textiles | 89 billion CM | Fine fabrics, uniforms |
| Metalwork | 52 billion CM | Tools, architectural elements |
| Craft Goods | 38 billion CM | Ceramics, woodwork, jewelry |
| Food Processing | 35 billion CM | Preserved goods, beverages |

### Services (37% of GDP)
The fastest-growing sector includes:

- **Tourism**: Cultural sites like the Great Library of Eldoria (opened 1289)
- **Education**: University system with 18 regional campuses
- **Financial Services**: Banking regulated by the Third Taper
- **Healthcare**: Mandatory public coverage system

## Taxation System

### Income Tax Brackets

| Income Range | Tax Rate | Notes |
|--------------|----------|-------|
| 0-5,000 Tapers | 0% | Personal allowance |
| 5,001-15,000 Tapers | 5% | Basic working class |
| 15,001-50,000 Tapers | 12% | Middle income |
| 50,001-100,000 Tapers | 22% | Upper middle |
| 100,001-500,000 Tapers | 35% | High income |
| Above 500,000 Tapers | 45% | Highest income bracket |

### Land Tax
A progressive tax on land ownership based on productivity:

$$ T_{land} = L_{size} \times Y_{yield} \times R_{rate} $$

- $L_{size}$: Land area in hectares
- $Y_{yield}$: Average crop yield per hectare
- $R_{rate}$: Tax rate (0.03 to 0.08)

## Economic Regulations

### The Merchant Protection Act (1312)
Protects foreign merchants and ensures:
- Equal treatment under Eldorian law
- Protection from arbitrary confiscation
- Right to appeal trade disputes

### The Harmony Index Calculation
The Harmony Index determines economic policy decisions:

$$ H = 0.3W + 0.25I + 0.2E + 0.15J + 0.05P $$

Where:
- $W$: Wealth distribution measure
- $I$: Innovation index
- $E$: Employment rate
- $J$: Job satisfaction survey
- $P$: Project completion rate

## Labor System

### Worker Rights
Eldorian workers are guaranteed:
- Minimum wage of 2,000 Tapers/month
- 48-hour maximum work week
- 30 days paid vacation annually
- Universal healthcare coverage

### Collective Bargaining
Unions are organized by industry and negotiate with:
1. Individual employers for company-specific terms
2. Industry councils for sector-wide standards
3. The Third Taper for national minimum standards
