# The Sovereign Concordat of Eldoria

## Overview
The Sovereign Concordat of Eldoria is a constitutional monarchy established in 1247 CE after the Unification Wars. Unlike typical monarchies, Eldoria operates under a unique **Dual-Rule System** where executive and legislative powers are divided between the Crown and the Council of Seven Tapers.

## Government Structure

### The Crown Chamber
The Crown holds ceremonial and symbolic authority while maintaining veto power over:
- Military declarations
- Economic sanctions against foreign nations
- Alterations to the Great Charter of Eldoria

### The Council of Seven Tapers
The legislative body consists of seven appointed members known as "Tapers" who symbolize illumination of different aspects of society:

| Taper Name | Symbol | Domain |
|------------|--------|--------|
| First Taper | The Lantern | Education & Arts |
| Second Taper | The Shield | Defense & Security |
| Third Taper | The Balance | Commerce & Trade |
| Fourth Taper | The Scale | Justice & Law |
| Fifth Taper | The Sprout | Agriculture & Land |
| Sixth Taper | The Wave | Industry & Innovation |
| Seventh Taper | The Hearth | Healthcare & Welfare |

### Local Administration
Eldoria is divided into 18 regions called **Cantons**, each governed by a **Regional Steward** appointed by the Central Concordat. These stewards hold regional assemblies that can pass local bylaws without federal approval, provided they do not contradict the Great Charter.

## Unique Governance Principles

### The Principle of Tides
Unlike fixed-term elections, Eldorian governance operates on a **Tidal Cycle** system:
$$ T_n = 5 \text{ years } + \Delta_{n} $$

Where $\Delta_{n}$ varies based on population metrics and economic indicators. Elections occur every 4 to 6 years depending on the **Harmony Index**, which is calculated from seven economic and social factors.

### The Silence Protocol
If the Council of Seven Tapers fails to reach consensus on critical legislation, the law enters a mandatory **Silence Period** of 30 days. During this time:
- No additional debate is permitted
- Public petitions must be submitted in writing only
- Media coverage is limited to factual reporting

## Historical Context

### The Unification Wars (1220-1247)
Three primary factions merged into the Concordat:
1. **The Northern Highland Confederates**: Mountain regions favoring tradition
2. **The Coastal Mercantile League**: Trade-focused port cities
3. **The River Valley Republics**: Agricultural central plains

### The Charter of Illumination (1289)
A constitutional amendment that created the First Taper position following the Great Library Fire of 1285 CE.
